Field Study 2: Participation in Teaching–Learning Process

Team Teaching Demonstration

During our team teaching demonstration, I collaborated with Ma’am Bles Pioquinto to deliver a comprehensive lesson focused on Ako ay Pilipino, dahil Pilipino ang aking Magulang and to know the Letter N.

My primary responsibility involved setting the stage for engagement by introducing the nursery rhyme, "Ako, Ikaw, Kita Tanan Isa Ka Komunidad," which reinforced the theme of unity. I also facilitated the motivational "Guess the Picture" activity to let the learners engage and prepare them for the core content of the session.

In the instructional phase, I led the reading of the story "Sinang-una nga mga Pilipino", providing the learners with a foundational look at early Filipino life. I developed classroom scenarios and used AI tools to make history immersive and tailored to student needs.

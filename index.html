WVSU - Integrated Laboratory School (WVSU-ILS)

Located on campus, the WVSU-ILS is the training department of the West Visayas State University College of Education. It serves as a "laboratory" where student teachers practice under expert supervision. The school consists of the Elementary Department (est. 1924) and the Secondary Department (est. 1969). Known for academic excellence, journalism, and sports, it blends traditional learning with experimental methods.

Graciano Lopez Jaena Elementary School (GLJES)

Located in La Paz, Iloilo City, GLJES is a historic public school named after the national hero. It was founded in 1938 and renamed in 1967. A central hub for the La Paz district, it hosts DepEd events and joins cultural activities like Dinagyang Festival. The school promotes K–12 education rooted in heritage and community.

Receiving Teacher (RT): Lilibeth Tantiado
A WVSU graduate of 1988, Ms. Tantiado brings decades of experience as a Kindergarten teacher nurturing early learners.
